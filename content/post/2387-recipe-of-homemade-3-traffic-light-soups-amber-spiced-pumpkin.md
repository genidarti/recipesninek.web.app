##### The ingredients needed to make 3 Traffic Light Soups - AMBER - Spiced Pumpkin:

1. Make ready 1 Cup Onion Diced
1. Take 1 Cup Chicken Stock
1. Take 1 Cup Milk
1. Prepare 1 Tsp Ground Ginger
