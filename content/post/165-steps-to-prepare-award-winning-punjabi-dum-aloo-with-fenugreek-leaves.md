##### The ingredients needed to make Punjabi Dum aloo with fenugreek leaves:

1. Prepare 6-7 potatoes peeled,pricked and soaked in salt water
1. Take As required Oil for cooking and frying
1. Get 2 tbsp curd
1. Make ready 7-8 broken cashews
1. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
1. Take to taste Salt
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp coriander powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp garam masala
1. Get 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
1. Take 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp kitchen king masala
1. Take 2 tbsp fenugreek leaves dried
1. Take 2 onions chopped
1. Take 5-6 tomatoes chopped
1. Prepare 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
1. Make ready 1 tsp onion seeds(kalongi)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Punjabi Dum aloo with fenugreek leaves:

1. Drain potatoes and fry in hot oil.
1. In a kadai, sauté finely chopped onions and ginger garlic paste in oil and then add tomatoes to it.
1. Season it with salt, pepper,turmeric And red chilli powder and Let them cook, till oil separates and then add curd into it. Mix well so that curd does not curdle.
1. Soak cashews in hot milk.
1. Now purée them and add in the kadai. This will thicken the gravy.
1. Cover and Let it cook for sometime.
1. Finally once cooled add fried potatoes to it and give it a mix.
1. Cover and cook for some time and then add coriander powder to it.
1. Finally add dried fenugreek leaves and onion seeds to enhance it&#39;s flavours.
1. Serve hot with roti or paranthas.
