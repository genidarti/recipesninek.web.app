##### Steps to make WorkShop Stuffed Falafel with two dipping sauce and Salsa:

1. For Falafel - Pour the chickpeas into a large bowl,Wash and soak in water for overnight.
1. Drain the water and keep aside for 1/2 an hour.
1. Now Take a large grinder jar add all the Falafel ingredients to a coarse mixture.
1. Once the mixture reaches the desired consistency, pour it into a bowl - You can leave it in the fridge for 20 minutes.
1. Put Chesse in a bowl and keep aside.
1. Apply oil on your hands. - Keep the falafel mixture on your hands. - Press it slightly.
1. Fill the stuffing into it and prepare a roll.
1. In this way, you can make all the falafel
1. Heat oil in a deep pan or kadhi and fry 1 ball to taste. If it falls apart, add a little flour.
1. Now fry about 4 balls at once for a few minutes on each side, or until golden brown. Drain on paper towel.
1. You can made approx 12 to 13 falafel to this mixture.
1. How to make Muhammara
1. Rinse the yellow Capcicum and pat dry. Brush it with little oil all over. Then Put the Capcicum directly on heat and roast, turning often for around 10 minutes until completely roasted. - Cover with plastic wrap for a few minutes when cool enough, simple peel the capsicum, remove the seeds and cut into small pieces.
1. For roasted Walnut - Heat the sesame oil in a pan. - add the walnut, use a spatula to stir the walnuts until they are nicely brown and deeply toasted flavour. -  it takes approx 5 minutes, now transfer to a tray. use when it cool down.
1. Take a grinder jar add Capcicum, garlic, cumin powder, lemon juice, roasted Walnuts, pomegranate molasses, Red chilli powder, chickpeas, sesame oil and salt.
1. Mix it well. - Transfer it to a bowl, if desired top with the drizzle of sesame oil.
1. For Baba Ganoush - Mix all the ingredients in a grinder and mix well then put in a bowl.
1. For Salsa - Take a large bowl and add all the Salsa ingredients and mix well.
1. Serve the falafel with Baba Ganoush, Muhammara and salsa.
