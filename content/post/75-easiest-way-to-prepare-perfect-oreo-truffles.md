##### The ingredients needed to make Oreo Truffles:

1. Prepare 1 package Oreos; crushed
1. Prepare 8 oz cream cheese; softened
1. Prepare 16 oz semi sweet chocolate; melted




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Oreo Truffles:

1. Reserve 1/8 of crushed cookies. Mix cream cheese and crushed oreos together. Freeze for 10-15 minutes.
1. Dip cookie balls into melted chocolate, and lay on a baking tray lined with parchment paper. Sprinkle remaining cookie crumbs on top. Place in fridge.
1. Variations; Other cookies, white chocolate, mexican chocolate, cinnamon, pumpkin, jalapeño powder, espresso, coffee, sprinkles, nuts, chiles
