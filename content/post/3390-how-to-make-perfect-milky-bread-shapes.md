##### The ingredients needed to make Milky Bread shapes:

1. Make ready 2 cups flour
1. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
1. Get 3 tbsp butter
1. Make ready 1 tbsp yeast
1. Take 4 tbsp milk powder
1. Take 3 eggs (1 for egg wash)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Milky Bread shapes:

1. Dissolve the yeast in warm water. Add sugar, milk &amp; eggs. Allow until bubble start to appear on the surface.
1. Pour the liquid into the flour and mix to form a slightly sticky dough
1. Place on a work surface &amp; knead for 4mins. Flatten out, add butter &amp; gradually work it into the dough. Knead until dough is no more sticky
1. Place dough in an oiled pan &amp; leave to rise for 1 hr. Then punch down to remove the air. Don&#39;t knead the dough. Cut out &amp; make into desired shapes. Place on a greased parchment paper pan. Leave to rise for 30 mins
1. Preheat the oven. Brush the bread before baking in the oven to get that glossy shine &amp; brown crust
