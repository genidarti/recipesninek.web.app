##### The ingredients needed to make Pasta Bake:

1. Prepare 1 lb ground beef
1. Make ready 16 oz pasta sauce
1. Make ready 1 medium onion, chopped
1. Get 1 green pepper chopped
1. Take 1 box pasta
1. Make ready 1 packages mozzarella cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Pasta Bake:

1. Cook ground beef, onion and green pepper together in a skillet until ground beef is brown.  Drain.
1. Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package directions.  Set aside.
1. Add pasta sauce to ground beef, onions and peppers; stir.
1. Once the meat and sauce are mixed, add pasta.  Thoroughly mix them together.
1. Grease a 9x13&#34; baking dish.  Add pasta mixture and spread evenly.  Top with cheese.  Bake in 350° oven for 30 min or until cheese is melted and mixture is heated through.  Enjoy!
