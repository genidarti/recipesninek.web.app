##### The ingredients needed to make Pain Perdu (French Toast):

1. Make ready 2 slice Bread
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Butter or vegetable oil
1. Prepare 1 Egg
1. Get 1 pinch Ground Nutmeg
1. Take 1/4 cup Sugar
1. Get 1/2 cup Milk
