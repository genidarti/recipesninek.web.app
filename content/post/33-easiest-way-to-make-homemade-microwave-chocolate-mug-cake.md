##### The ingredients needed to make Microwave Chocolate Mug Cake:

1. Get 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1. Make ready 1/4 tbsp sugar
1. Take 2 tbsp cocoa powder
1. Make ready 1/8 tbsp baking soda
1. Make ready 1/8 tsp salt
1. Make ready 1 tbsp milk
1. Get 2 tbsp oil
1. Take As required Choco chips
1. Take As required Chocolate syrup




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Microwave Chocolate Mug Cake:

1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in a mug. Now stir the batter in milk in one direction.
1. Apply the oil to the sides of the mug. Pour the batter to the mug and microwave it for 1-2 minutes.
1. Once it is fluffy you can add choco chips for decoration. You can also pour chocolate syrup.
